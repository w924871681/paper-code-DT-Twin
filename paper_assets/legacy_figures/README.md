# Unchanged paper figure assets

These seven PDFs are the unchanged release assets used by the revised paper.
Level B verifies their committed SHA-256 values and copies them into the
complete paper-output directory. They are preserved instead of being redrawn,
because this revision did not authorize changes to their scientific content.

The five figures added or redesigned in v1.1.0 are different: they are
regenerated by `reporting/frozen.py`, and each data-driven figure is derived
from released frozen CSV/JSON results. `fig2.pdf` was normalized without a
visual-content change so that standard PDF readers can parse it; both its
pre-normalization and released hashes are recorded in `manifest.json`.

The legacy assets predate the v1.1.0 figure workflow and may contain embedded
raster elements. The five revised figures are the artifacts subject to the
strict all-vector, 600-DPI PNG, grayscale, and layout checks.
