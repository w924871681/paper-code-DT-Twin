# Historical paper figure assets

This directory preserves seven historical PDFs and their committed SHA-256
values. Level B still verifies and copies Fig. 2--5 unchanged. The historical
Fig. 6, Fig. 8, and Fig. 9 PDFs remain here for provenance comparison but are
superseded in generated and manuscript outputs by public-CSV reconstructions.

Eight figures are now regenerated by `reporting/frozen.py`, and each
data-driven figure is derived from released CSV/JSON results. `fig2.pdf` was normalized without a
visual-content change so that standard PDF readers can parse it; both its
pre-normalization and released hashes are recorded in `manifest.json`.

The historical assets predate the v1.1.0 figure workflow and may contain
embedded raster elements. The eight generated figures are the artifacts
subject to the strict all-vector, 600-DPI PNG, grayscale, and layout checks.
